Welcome to TOSSPI: The One Stop Shopping Place.

Our goal is to be able to offer new and used products to our customers at an affordable price.

We are based out of West Valley City, UT. We desire to offer a platform similar to Ebay, Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, etc. We all know that online retail is primarily where products are now bought and sold. Our mission is to have individuals and companies that want to sell their merchandise online is to ship it directly to us and we will do all of the leg work for you. We will check in your products you send us. We will test the products if they are previously owned to ensure they are in working order. we will take pictures of the products, list the products, sell and ship the products. We will also handle all of the returns. We will deal with the positive and negative aspects of the retail selling. As we grow our plan is to purchase the products from you directly as we receive them. This way you have more time to do the things in life you enjoy doing. Initially, we will recieve the products and then sell them for you directly. We will charge a percentage of 15% of the sales price as our fee. There is no monthly fee like other online retailers require to sell on thier sites. Thank you for taking the time to read a little information about our company and what our business model has to offer.
